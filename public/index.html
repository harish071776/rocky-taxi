FAQ
How do I cancel a ride? ▾
Go to Home, tap the active ride banner, and press "Cancel ride".
What is the OTP for? ▾
Share the 4-digit OTP with your captain when they arrive to confirm you're the right passenger.
How does live location sharing work? ▾
When you book a ride, you can tap 📡 to share your GPS with the captain in real-time.
When is fare credited to captain? ▾
The moment the captain taps "Complete Ride", the fare is instantly credited to their earnings wallet.
